Course Overview

Our AP Computer Science Principles course is designed to introduce students to the foundational concepts of computer science and computational thinking. Through engaging lessons and practical exercises, students will develop a thorough understanding of computing, programming, data analysis, and the societal impacts of technology.

Class Structure

  • Weekly 1-hour live classes
  • Exploration of key concepts in computing and programming
  • Hands-on coding exercises and projects
  • Exam preparation with quizzes and practice exams

Who Should Join?

This course is ideal for students who are planning to take the AP Computer Science Principles exam. No prior experience in computer science is required, making it accessible to all students with an interest in technology and computing.
