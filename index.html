A better way to learn computer science.

Experienced Teachers

Each and every one of our coaches is a software professional with at least twenty years of industry experience and, thus, a great deal of knowledge in and passion for the field of computer science. Not only that, but all of our coaches have over a decade of experience teaching middle and high school students to code.

Personalized Instruction

Our small class sizes (3-6 students in each group) ensure that coaches can cater to the individual needs and learning styles of each student. Not only that, but our "ongoing learning" offering allows you to receive one-on-one innstruction on whichever language, concept, or application you are seeking to develop your skills in.

Hands-on Learning

As individuals with decades of industry experience, our coaches understand the necessity of applying theoretical knowledge/concepts to solve real-world problems. Accordingly, in every course, students will be continuously challenged with practical problems to solve based on their learnings.

Quality Content

Honed over years and years of coaching students, our content is primed to develop young students' passion for and skills in the field of computer science. Importantly, apart from just providing students with great breadth and depth of knowledge in whichever language they've chosen to learn, we seek to instill in them problem-solving skills which will prepare them for future endeavors.
